Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about FoodExpress

How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our menu, select your desired items, add them to cart, and proceed to checkout. You can also use our search feature to find specific dishes or restaurants.

Can I modify my order after placing it?

You can modify your order within 2 minutes of placing it. After that, the restaurant begins preparation and modifications may not be possible. Contact customer support for assistance.

How long does delivery take?

Most orders are delivered within 30-45 minutes. Delivery time may vary based on your location, restaurant preparation time, and weather conditions. You can track your order in real-time.

What are the delivery charges?

Delivery charges are ₹49 for most orders. We offer free delivery on orders above ₹500. Special promotions may apply for reduced or free delivery charges.

Can I track my order?

Yes! You can track your order in real-time through our website or app. You'll receive updates when your order is confirmed, being prepared, picked up, and delivered.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, UPI payments, net banking, digital wallets, and cash on delivery. All online transactions are secure and encrypted.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your payment information. We never store your complete card details on our servers.

What if my payment fails?

If your payment fails, you can try again with the same or different payment method. If the issue persists, contact your bank or our customer support team for assistance.

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button and provide your name, email, and phone number. You can also sign up using your Google or Facebook account for faster registration.

Can I change my delivery address?

Yes, you can add multiple delivery addresses in your account settings. You can select or change your delivery address during checkout.

Do you have minimum order requirements?

Minimum order requirements vary by restaurant and location. You'll see the minimum order amount on the restaurant page before placing your order.

What if my order is delayed or missing?

If your order is significantly delayed or missing items, please contact our customer support immediately. We'll work with the restaurant to resolve the issue and may offer compensation.

Can I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order within 2 minutes of placing it for a full refund. After preparation begins, cancellation may not be possible, but please contact support for assistance.

How do I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support through live chat, email at info@foodexpress.com, or phone at +91 1234567890. Our support team is available 24/7 to assist you.

Still have questions?

Our customer support team is here to help you 24/7
